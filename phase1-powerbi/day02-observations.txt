Dataset Name: Student_dataset.csv

Number of rows: 50

Number of columns: 7

Column meanings:
- StudentID: unique number given to each student
- Name: name of the student
- Age: age of the student
- Gender: male or female
- Course: which course the student is studying
- Marks: marks scored by the student
- Attendance: attendance percentage of the student

One surprising thing:
I noticed that most students who have high marks also have high attendance, so attendance might be affecting their performance.
